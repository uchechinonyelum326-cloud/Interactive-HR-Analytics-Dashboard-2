# Interactive-HR-Analytics-Dashboard-2
Power BI HR Dashboard: Employee Analytics and Visualization, Advanced Data Analysis Project.
This Power BI HR Dashboard provides a full insight into a workforce of 1,470 total employees.Key Breakdown:Gender: 882 Male (60%) and 588 Female (40%)Work Status: 1,012 Active workers (68.8%) and 458 Non-Active workers (31.2%). Promotion: 1,169 employees (79.5%) are due for promotion, while 301 (20.5%) are not due. Retirement: 5 employees (0.3%) are near retirement. Service Years: Highest counts are 5 years (196 employees) and 1 year (171 employees). Job Levels: Majority are in Level 1 (543) and Level 2 (534). Distance Status: 940 employees (63.95%) are "Very close", 301 are "Close", and 229 (15.58%) are "Far". The dashboard uses KPI cards, bar charts, and a donut chart to visualize employee distribution, promotion readiness, tenure, and location status.
